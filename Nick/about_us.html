Building on 250 years of local watchmaking history, the dramatic landscape of Scotland and its industrial heritage inspire Kartel’s approach to watchmaking, inspired and sustained by a singular aim.
To create a truly refined modern classic.

It began with the desire to instil the values of a rich watchmaking past into a product while being mindful of our roots, our history, heritage and culture.

An agile, independent company Kartel can follow its heart in the design and production of timepieces. Graduating from a centre of internationally recognised design excellence. Kartel’s designers approach their work with a rare passion. All products are designed in Scotland with each watch borne of a meticulous design process.
