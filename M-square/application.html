We design and engineer a range of photonics systems, which we supply to industry all over the world. We also partner with businesses to create unique applications and solve challenging problems. Our tools have been used in a wide range of sectors, from frontier science to space, defence, oil and gas, healthcare and food and drink.

Enabling


THE PIONEERS

FRONTIER SCIENCE

Our laser and photonics applications are used by scientists working at the cutting edge of discovery. Used in laboratories across the world, our tools help to investigate nature beyond the existing paradigms of science, enabling breakthroughs and facilitating groundbreaking experiments.

PROPELLING

THE CURIOUS

SPACE

Our lasers are helping overcome the fuel-intensive nature of space travel, which is a major obstacle for those looking beyond our solar system. We helped develop the first ion-drive – the next generation in space propulsion – which is enabling travel to new and unexplored worlds.

PREVENTING

THE UNAVOIDABLE

FOOD & DRINK

Our pioneering apparatus and monitoring system prevents the fluid loss during maturation that whiskey makers had long found unavoidable. Saving money for the makers and depriving the angels of their share.

IDENTIFYING

THE THREATENING

SECURITY & DEFENSE

Our Firefly technology detects harmful chemical leaks at great distances, saving lives and defusing threats in some of the world’s most challenging environments.

PROTECTING

THE VALUABLE

OIL & GAS

Our Firefly technology reveals details hidden to the naked eye, allowing users to detect gas and oil leaks before they cause irreparable damage to our environment.

DIAGNOSING

THE INVISIBLE

HEALTHCARE

Our Firefly-IR lasers can detect the bio-molecules in human breath that hold the key to diagnosing conditions from cancer to liver and kidney failure, opening the possibility of a radical shift to noninvasive health screening.
