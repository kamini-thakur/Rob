VISIT M SQUARED AND JOIN


a WORKSHOP IN MUNICH


FROM 26 - 29 JUNE

  1. Home
  2. News & Events

We’ll be on the road at the end of June, visiting our customers and collaborators as we make our way to Laser World of Photonics in Germany.

Representatives from our scientific laser and innovation teams will be taking to the trusty M Squared Landrover and heading for Munich, where the biennial trade show and the conference will take place.

The trip gives us a chance to find out how some of our long-term customers and collaborators are doing, catch up with their latest work.

Once we’ve made some visits, we’ll be arriving at the trade show, which runs from 26 - 29 June.

We'd love to see you at Laser World of Photonics - we’ll be exhibiting some of our innovative photonics tools and giving a range of Workshops on our stand:

Make a quantum leap with SolsTiS

  • An insight into M Squared’s award-winning SolsTiS CW narrow linewidth laser and how it is shaping quantum science.
  • Runs 10 - 10:15 am and 12 - 12:15 pm daily
