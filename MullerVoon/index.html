About MullerVoon

MullerVoon was established by Tani Muller and Christine Voon in September 2016.
A small but tightknit team of two, located in Sydney Australia.
We aim to provide artwork that complements and enriches your home or office.

Frames Canvas and Materials

All artwork that we provide are created using the finest materials and quality.
All photo prints degrade in colour over time, this is dependant largely on environmental conditions.
However, using museum grade materials and quality, our prints will not begin to fade for at least 75 years or even longer.

Print paper solutions

We use the best available paper/canvas solutions. Currently 310 gsm 100% cotton rag, acid free, is one of the best. 75 years fade free is good but we never rest on our laurels; we will always be watching industry for even better solutions

Ink technology & Approaches

We use acid free ink, from canon, using 12 colour ink cartridge. Interestingly enough this ink is worth about its own weight in gold..!!

Frame solutions & Support

Our frames our acid free and we use acrylic panes instead of glass for a number of reasons, even though glass is cheaper to use; Acrylic is lighter than glass and does not shatter or crack should the frame be dropped, the acrylic pane also has a high UV rating, which better protects the artwork from color fading
